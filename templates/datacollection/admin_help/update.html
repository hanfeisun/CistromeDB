How to update a paper entry

  1. go to the papers page; find the paper you want to edit; click the "edit" btn
  2. alternatively, if you know the paper's id, go to the following page: http://cistrome.org/newdc/update_paper_form/NNN/ where NNN is the paper's id
  3. edit out the form
  4. click submit
