How to delete paper entries

  1. go to the papers page; check the boxes of the papers you want to delete (check the first box to select all of the papers on that page); click the "delete" btn
  2. confirm or cancel

How to delete dataset entries

  1. go to the datasets page; check the boxes of the datasets you want to delete (check the first box to select all of the datasets on that page); click the "delete" btn
  2. confirm or cancel

How to delete sample entries

  1. go to the samples page; check the boxes of the samples you want to delete (check the first box to select all of the samples on that page); click the "delete" btn
  2. confirm or cancel
